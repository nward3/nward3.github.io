About Me

I am from Erie, Pennsylvania and I'm currently a senior at University of Notre Dame studying Computer Science. I am fascinated with all aspects of computer science and I enjoy constantly learning and expanding my knowledge of the tech world. I am particularly interested in the intersection of computer science with various fields and the results of these joint applications. While I like to spend time learning more computer science things, I also like to dabble other material such as foreign languages and business concepts. I hope to use my computer science background in conjunction with my diverse interests to positively impact people through technological empowerment. Outside of my studies, I enjoy playing tennis, reading, playing piano, listening to music, traveling, and hanging out with friends.
