about me

I am from Erie, Pennsylvania and I'm currently a junior at University of Notre Dame studying Computer Science.

I am fascinated with all aspects of computer science and I enjoy constantly learning and expanding my knowledge of the tech world. I am particularly interested in seeing the joint application of computer science with various different fields and the results of these intersections. While I like to spend time learning more computer science things, I also like to dabble other material such as foreign languages (I'm currently studying Italian) or business concepts. Additionally, I enjoy playing tennis, reading, playing piano, listening to music, traveling, and hanging out with friends.

Next summer I will be working for Amazon Robotics as a software engineer intern in Boston. Previously, I had the opportunity to intern as a software engineer for Erie Insurance in my hometown.

projects

eatXpress

My squad and I developed a service for ordering and delivering food that is mobile oriented but works for both mobile and desktop devices. Our service enables people who want some grub to post a bounty price to have food bought and delivered, while the go-getters of the world carry out the orders, pocketing the difference between the order cost and the associated bounty. We developed eatXpress using the MEAN stack.

the odyssey

While interning at Erie Insurance with the Production Support Team, I built a web application for organizing and managing the team's numerous applications. This tool was developed using SQL Server and C# with the .NET framework.
